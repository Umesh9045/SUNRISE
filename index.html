Best Education

Sunrise Science Institute is a leading science institute dedicated to providing high-quality education in field of science. Our experienced and qualified faculty members are committed to helping students achieve their full potential and excel in their chosen fields. We offer a wide range of courses, all designed to provide students with a strong foundation in field of science and prepare them for successful careers. Our institute ensures that our students receive an education that meets the highest standards.

Top Level

Our management team is composed of experienced and dedicated professionals who are committed to advancing scientific research and discovery. Our team members possess a diverse range of skills and expertise, and work collaboratively to achieve our institute’s goals. We value trust, vision, self-awareness and emotional intelligence, leadership, mentoring, team evolution and dynamics, communication, recognition and sharing success, conflict and disagreement management, and navigating and leveraging networks and systems. Together, we strive to create an environment that fosters innovation, collaboration, and excellence.

Quality Meeting

Our parents’ meeting is an opportunity for parents to meet with teachers and staff to discuss their child’s progress and development. During the meeting, we provide updates on academic performance, behavior, and social-emotional growth. We also discuss any concerns or questions that parents may have and work together to develop strategies to support their child’s success. Our goal is to foster open communication and collaboration between parents and teachers to ensure the best possible outcomes for our students
